Basic Pizza Recipe

To make a delicious homemade pizza

Ingredients:

  • Dough:
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (43°C)
  • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Sauce:
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toppings:
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Make the Dough:
    • Dissolve yeast in warm water with sugar. Let sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large bowl, mix flour and salt. Create a well in the center and add yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir until dough forms.
    • Knead dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for about 1-1.5 hours or until doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • Mix tomato sauce with oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Punch down the dough and divide into two portions. Roll out each portion on a floured surface to desired thickness.
    • Transfer dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
    • Spread sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges. Sprinkle with cheese and add your desired toppings.
  4. Bake the Pizza:
    • Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
    • Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
